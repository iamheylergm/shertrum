Vision

Shertrum will be a crypto technology and treasury company specializing in Bitcoin and Ripple, with the goal of protecting, managing, and optimizing capital in digital assets and developing innovative technological solutions in the blockchain ecosystem. The ultimate vision is for Shertrum to be not only a capital manager, but also an influential player in the sector, with its own community and a corporate token that strategically interacts with treasury assets to generate returns for investors and the company.

Learn about economics

Here you will find basic concepts and also information about the economic and financial world.

Economy

¿What is economics?

Economics is the system by which a society organizes the use of its resources to produce, distribute, and exchange goods and services in order to satisfy its needs. It studies how decisions are made about what to produce, how to produce it, and for whom, considering that resources are limited and needs are virtually unlimited. Essentially, economics explains how money, labor, businesses, and consumption function within a community or country.

Financial Markets

¿Because financial markets exist.?

Financial markets exist to allow money to flow between those who have capital and those who need it. They function as a meeting point where businesses, governments, and individuals can obtain financing to grow, invest, or develop projects, while investors can place their money with the expectation of making a profit. In essence, they facilitate investment, the efficient allocation of resources, and economic growth.

Digital Financial Marketplace

¿What is a digital financial marketplace?

A digital financial market is an online environment where financial assets are bought and sold through technological platforms, without the need for a physical space. It allows trading in stocks, currencies, cryptocurrencies, and other instruments from anywhere in the world via the internet, facilitating fast transactions, global access, and greater efficiency in capital exchange.

Volatility and Liquidity

¿What are volatility and liquidity?

Volatility is the degree to which an asset's price changes over a given period; the faster and more sharply its price rises or falls, the greater its volatility. Liquidity, on the other hand, is the ease with which that asset can be bought or sold without significantly affecting its price; a highly liquid market allows for quick entry and exit, while a low-liquidity market can generate sharp price movements.

Financial Asset

¿What is a financial asset?

A financial asset is an instrument that represents economic value and can generate future benefits for its owner. It can be a stock, a bond, a currency, or a cryptocurrency, and its value depends on supply, demand, and market expectations. Essentially, it's a way to invest or protect capital with the intention of generating returns or preserving value over time.

Trading

¿What is Trading?

Trading is the activity of buying and selling financial assets, such as stocks, currencies, or cryptocurrencies, with the goal of profiting from price movements. It involves analyzing the market, identifying opportunities, and executing trades over various timeframes, whether short, medium, or long term. Essentially, trading seeks to capitalize on market fluctuations to generate returns.

Shares

¿What are shares?

A share is a part or fraction of ownership of a company that is bought and sold on the financial market. By acquiring a share, the investor becomes a partner in the company in proportion to the amount they own, and can benefit from its appreciation or from dividend payments. Its price fluctuates according to supply, demand, and the company's performance in the market.

Cryptocurrency

¿What is a cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a digital asset that uses cryptographic technology and decentralized networks, such as blockchain, to ensure security and transparency in transactions. It does not depend on banks or governments to operate and allows for the direct sending and receiving of value between individuals over the internet. Its value is determined by supply, demand, and market confidence.

Blockchain

¿What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a distributed and secure database that records transactions publicly and immutably. It functions as a digital ledger where each block of information is linked to the previous one, guaranteeing transparency, security, and trust without the need for intermediaries. It is primarily used to support cryptocurrencies and other decentralized digital systems.

Bitcoin

¿What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized cryptocurrency that allows users to send and receive money securely and directly over the internet, without the need for banks or intermediaries. It operates on blockchain technology, which guarantees transparency, immutability, and control over transactions. Its value depends on supply, demand, and user confidence in the market.
