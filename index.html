Shertrum Technologies Inc.

Shertrum is focused on developing technologies that add value to the world of cryptoassets, including treasury of digital assets such as Bitcoin, Ripple, Ethereum, Hedera, and others that will be added over time. We look forward to growing alongside you and taking our products to the top of the industry.

Vision

Shertrum will be a crypto technology and treasury company specializing in Bitcoin and Ripple, with the goal of protecting, managing, and optimizing capital in digital assets and developing innovative technological solutions in the blockchain ecosystem. The ultimate vision is for Shertrum to be not only a capital manager, but also an influential player in the sector, with its own community and a corporate token that strategically interacts with treasury assets to generate returns for investors and the company.

Roadmap

Phase 1 – Foundation

  • • Define legal and regulatory structure (pending).
  • • Establish custody and security processes for Bitcoin and other assets.
  • • Create a strong corporate identity.
  • • Start online community.
  • • Attract early investors interested in the company's long-term vision.

Phase 2 – Development

  • • Crypto treasury tracking and auditing tool.
  • • Payment or remittance platform with Bitcoin and other assets.
  • • Automated investment solution with blockchain transparency.
  • • Develop and launch an MVP (minimum viable product).
  • • Validate with the community and adjust.

Phase 3 – Token

  • • Issue corporate tokens (with clear utility, not just speculative).
  • • Design economic interaction between the token and treasury assets.
  • • Partial backing of the token by reserves in Bitcoin and other hard assets.
  • • Profit sharing by token holding.
  • • Exclusive access to products or services.
  • • Liquidity strategy and exchange listing.

Initial model for investors

In the first stage, the appeal for investors will not just be to "earn from the rise of a token," but to participate in a business model backed by highly liquid assets, gain exposure to Bitcoin and XRP without directly managing them, access to the technological products we launch before the public, and future benefits linked to the token and its relationship with the treasury.

₿ Bitcoin

¿Qué es Bitcoin?

Bitcoin es una moneda digital descentralizada creada en 2009 por una persona o grupo bajo el seudónimo Satoshi Nakamoto. Funciona sin necesidad de bancos ni intermediarios, usando tecnología blockchain para registrar todas las transacciones de forma transparente y segura.

Ξ Ethereum

¿Qué es Ethereum?

Ethereum es una plataforma blockchain descentralizada que permite crear y ejecutar contratos inteligentes y aplicaciones descentralizadas (dApps). Su moneda nativa es el Ether (ETH), utilizada para pagar las operaciones dentro de la red.

✦ Ripple (XRP)

¿Qué es Ripple?

Ripple es una red de pagos digitales y protocolo de cambio de divisas. Su criptomoneda, XRP, está diseñada para facilitar transferencias internacionales de dinero de forma rápida y con comisiones muy bajas, siendo usada principalmente por instituciones financieras.

◈ Hedera (HBAR)

¿Qué es Hedera?

Hedera es una red pública de registro distribuido que utiliza una tecnología llamada hashgraph en lugar de blockchain tradicional. Su token nativo HBAR se usa para pagar servicios en la red, que destaca por su alta velocidad, bajo consumo energético y seguridad.
